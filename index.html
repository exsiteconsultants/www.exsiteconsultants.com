Advise

Maybe you've had an idea, maybe you want to try something out or just want a chat to understand if your idea is feasible or bonkers.

Alternatively, you know you need to build something but not sure how to organise your project or what technology to use. Exsite can advise on approaches and provide technical guidance with regards to what services to use or how to architect your application.

Prototype

You might have an idea, but you're not a "techy". Exsite can create a prototype before you commit the time and money needed to build the real thing.

A prototype can take the form of sketches, a slideshow or a very basic version of your service. Prototyping tests your ideas and allows you to put something in front of users early. Your users become part of the process instead of having a new application dumped on them at the end that doesn't take advantage of their experience or meet their needs.

Build

You know what you want to build but need help building it. Exsite has experience developing solid reliable services that work, backed by tests and monitoring tools that let you know if your application needs attention before your users tell you.

Exsite will help pick the best solution, using a reputable cloud ecommerce platform or a bespoke service that can safely and securely serve your users.
