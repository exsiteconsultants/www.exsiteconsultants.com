Advise

Maybe you've had an idea, maybe you want to try something out or just have a chat to understand if what you want to do is feasible or bonkers.

Alternatively you know you need to build something but not sure how to organise your project or what technology to use. Exsite can advise on user lead approaches to running your project or provide technical guidance with respect to what services to use or how to architect your application.

Prototype

You might have an idea, but you're not a "techy". Exsite can create a prototype to explore your ideas out before you commit to the time and cost needed to build the real thing.

A prototype can take the form of sketches, a slideshow or a very basic version of your service. This approach proves your idea and allows you to put something in front of your users. Your users become part of the process instead of having a new application dumped on them that doesn't take advantage of their experience or meet their needs.

Build

You know what you want to build but need help building it. Exsite has experience building solid reliable services that work, backed by tests and monitoring tools that let you know if your service needs attention before your users tell you.

Exsite will help decide the best solution, be it be on a reputable ecommerce platform that knows how to handle online transactions or a cloud based custom build service that can safely and securely serve your users.
