Sky Search

Exsite was responsible for the development of many projects, starting with the redevelopment of search for the Sky help website, and improvement of all of Sky’s web based search services.

As part of a Sky wide project to make their sites more responsive, and the introduction of a new search backend, Exsite was asked to re-write the search sites so that they could integrate with a new backend and provide support for a wide range of devices, from mobile up to desktop. I developed the new system with a NodeJS backend, which allowed fast flexible development and is capable of handling very high volumes of traffic in a fault tolerant configuration.

Some applications developed were single page web applications, whilst others followed a more traditional route, depending on their needs. Exsite also developed prototypes and proof of concept applications including a fully working mockup of ‘set top box search’ to gain buy in from the board as well as an iPad prototype to demonstrate future search capabilities on mobile devices.
